What is Staking?

Staking involves locking up funds in a decentralized protocol. In return, stakers receive yields either in the form of additional tokens or through an increase in the value of their staked tokens.

What is DUST?

DUST is a native token of DeDust.io, aimed at distributing the protocol's earnings via token staking.

How are rewards distributed?

The rewards for staking DUST were funded through the distribution of fees earned by DeDust.io until May 9, 2025.

Now the rewards distribution is delayed. To make it regular and more beneficial, our team works on a new version of staking. After its release in Q3 of 2025, all stakers will be rewarded with 200 000 DUST for the period since May 10, 2025.

Make note: the receiving amount of rewards will depend on the period during which your tokens were staked since May 10, 2025. Even if you don't have staked funds at the time of distribution, you will still be rewarded for the period when they were staked.

How can I unstake my DUST tokens?

Click Unstake and choose how to withdraw your funds. You can unstake them immediately with a 5% penalty or wait 72 hours to unstake without a service fee. A network fee is charged regardless of the chosen way of unstaking. It's a mandatory payment for processing your transaction in the blockchain.

Make note: along with the chosen amount of your tokens, the proportional amount of rewards will also be withdrawn.
